About Us

For nearly a decade, Progressive Kids Academy has pioneered Montessori education and maintained its unique and enviable identity. Established on 31st Jan 2009 by Mrs. Mamta Pandey, the academy focuses on the all-around development of every child, with motherly care as its hallmark.

We celebrate various festivals and events like Holi, Diwali, Rakshabandhan, Janmashtami, Sawan Mahotsav, Christmas, Food Festivals, Picnics, Birthdays, Independence Day, and Republic Day to promote cultural understanding and joy.

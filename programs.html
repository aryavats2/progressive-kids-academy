Programs We Offer

Playgroup

Ages: 2-3 years. Activities focused on sensory and motor development, social interaction, and foundational learning.

Nursery

Ages: 3-4 years. Programs designed to introduce early literacy, numeracy, and creative exploration.

Kindergarten

Ages: 4-5 years. A structured curriculum that prepares children for primary school, emphasizing cognitive and emotional growth.

Festivals and Activities

We celebrate various cultural and national festivals such as Raksha Bandhan, Holi, Diwali, and Independence Day to instill a sense of tradition and joy in children. Each celebration is filled with fun activities, crafts, and performances that help kids learn about the importance of these occasions.

Our school also organizes summer camps where children can explore art, music, dance, and sports. These camps are designed to provide a balance of learning and recreation.

Additionally, we host summer trips to nearby parks, museums, and historical landmarks, offering an opportunity for experiential learning and building memorable experiences.
